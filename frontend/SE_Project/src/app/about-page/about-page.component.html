// THE GRID

A research collaboration platform connecting grad students, postdocs, and faculty across universities worldwide.

[ OUR MISSION ]

The Grid breaks down institutional barriers so researchers can collaborate freely across disciplines, share ideas, and create impactful work together. We believe interdisciplinary discovery starts with open academic networks.

■

Project Collaboration

Create, manage, and join collaborative research projects with ease.

■

Search & Discovery

Find collaborators and projects based on expertise and interests.

■

Cross-Institution Networking

Build connections beyond your university and discipline.

■

Smart Invitations

Invite teammates by role and manage collaboration requests in-app.
